Inverse LaPlace Transform

When do we care about this?

This is using the Table to go backwards on LaPlace transforms. The analog is like looking at the table of integrals, and finding what the derivative must have been.

THE ONLY ISSUE is that these will not look exactly like what is in the table of our transforms. So much of the work, as usual, is the algebra to get it into a workable form. Sound familiar??

Again, the point of all of this is to familiarize ourselves with using the table, so this tool becomes worthwhile in solving ODEs. We are not there yet, but we are close!

What do we need to remember?

Mostly? Partial Fraction Decomposition. And factoring. And all of algebra.
