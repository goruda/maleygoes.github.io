"LaPlace Transform"

When do we care about this?

When DE's involve discontinuities or piecewise functions, OR when the DE is complicated enough that transforming it into an algebra problem would be preferable. A common application is transforming the perspective of a model from time-based functions to frequency-based functions.

What do we need to remember?

This is a brand new idea, so really not too much will be helpful to review. Knowing that you already have seen Transforms/Operators before (Like the Differentiation Operator) will help this feel less... disorienting.
