Getting Stuck

Online classes are tough because I can't read your face to see if you're stuck. And it is a matter of time before that happens!
That means you need to advocate for yourself (a great skill to get better at.) Please use the Class Discussion to ask questions of me and your classmates. This is where I respond the fastest, as I prioritize this part of the course over other things.

A Note on Time Expectations

On campus, a class session is about 2 hours, twice a week. Then another 8 hours per week is expected in study and practice. So you're looking at 12 hours per week, just for this class. (This is by the NYS level definition of what credit hours mean by the way!)

This can be a shock to those of us, especially working full time. But a 12 credit hour schedule means you are dedicating 36 hours a week just to school. So make sure to use your time wisely!!
