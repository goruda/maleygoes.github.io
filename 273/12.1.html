Geometry in 3D, Vectors (12.1) and Course Overview

Why Do We Care

First of all, I hope you care about the overview of the course, so we know where we're headed!

More specifically about the topics today though: Vectors are a more general way to represent relationships, and it turns out that they can trivialize a lot of problems that are hard if you don't use vectors. Also, what do our 2D formulas look like in 3D? If you're going to be working in 3D space, or using anything with more than 2 dimensions, you will care about this.
