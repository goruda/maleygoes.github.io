Triple Integrals in Spherical and Cylindrical Coordinates

What we'll learn

  1. The theory behind triple integrals in spherical and cylindrical coordinates.
  2. How to convert and compute triple integrals in these new coordinate systems.
  3. Applications of triple integrals in physical and geometrical problems.
