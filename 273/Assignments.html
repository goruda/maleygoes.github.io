MAT 273 Assignments and Assessments

Textbook Practice Problems

I want to give you credit for practicing diligently. There is no way to learn math without doing math.

These are graded on a rubric based on how neat and complete they are. You have the answers to all of them, so really I want to see your learning process and approaches. If you get stuck, which we all will, use the Class Discussion Miro Board and we can tackle it together. If you have more than a couple problems missing from these, you should get in touch with me ASAP.

See the pacing guide and *stick with it*. You cannot possibly rush these near the deadline, as some of these assignments could take hours. You should be working on these sets daily, and then combining them all at the end of the module. I cannot stress this enough. These are due right before the exam on each module.

  • Module 2 Practice Assignments: 5.1, 5.2, 5.3, 5.4, 5.5, 5.6, 5.7
